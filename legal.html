Legal

The art on this site is intended to be free from copyright due to the reasons below:

  • Images are presented with "fair use" under Section 107 of the copyright act 1976, due to the educational and non-profit nature of this website.
  • Under the Berne Convention, works of art by artists who have long been deceased lose their copyright with varying copyright lengths death depending upon the work's country of origin.
  • According to Bridgeman Art Library v. Corel Corp., unoriginal pictures of works of art in the public domain can't be copyrighted.
