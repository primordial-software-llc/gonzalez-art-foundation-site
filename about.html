About

The Gonzalez Art Foundation is dedicated to the preservation and proliferation of art. The foundation was founded by Timothy Gonzalez after he identified a need to more efficiently view hundreds of thousands of images.

Board of Directors

  • Timothy Scott Gonzalez
  • Kiara Elsa Manrique Loyola

Preservation

The Gonzalez Art Foundation accomplishes its mission of preservation by crawling, classifying and archiving digital works of art that are publicly accessible.

Proliferation

The Gonzalez Art Foundation accomplishes its mission of proliferation by presenting art in a minimalist gallery. The image viewer is capable of presenting one image statically or quickly presenting many images one-by-one in a slideshow.

Images are presented with "fair use" under Section 107 of the copyright act 1976, due to the educational and non-profit nature of this website. Furthermore the majority of the works presented by this website were created by artists who have been deceased for over 100 years and fall under the Bernie Convention and are free from copyright.
